Features

Embracing the needs of its residents, the hostel offers an array of features including communal spaces for socializing, study areas for academic pursuits, and convenient amenities like laundry facilities, ensuring a comfortable and enriching experience for all.

Three cozy hostels on campus offer convenient accommodations, unique amenities, and a vibrant community for students. Amidst the campus hostels, one beckons with the allure of air-conditioned comfort, while the others foster a vibrant community spirit in their non-AC abodes. Within the hostel's welcoming embrace, rooms embrace the joy of companionship with cozy 3/2 sharing arrangements, forging bonds and creating memories that endure.
